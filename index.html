That's Not My Neighbor v1.1.0.0

That's not my neighbor just got a spooky update! Version 1.1.0.0 introduces a brand new Nightmare Mode alongside some general improvements.

Nightmare Mode

The main attraction of this update is definitely the Nightmare Mode. This new difficulty mode is designed to crank up the challenge and scare factor. Here's what you can expect:

  • More Chilling Doppelgangers: Get ready for some seriously creepy neighbors! In Nightmare Mode, the doppelgangers will appear even more distorted and unsettling than before.
  • Intensified Gameplay: The atmosphere will be thicker and the scares will come faster. Brace yourself for a truly suspenseful and frightening experience.
  • On top of the new Nightmare Mode, the update also includes some general bug fixes and performance improvements to make the game run smoother.

If you're a fan of That's not my neighbor, this update is a must-try. Just be warned, Nightmare Mode isn't for the faint of heart! Nacho Sama, the developer, even warns that it's not recommended for new players [2].
